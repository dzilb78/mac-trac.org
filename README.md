# MacTrac- AI Macro Tracking Extension
Website Base Files

MacTrac is an AI-powered Chrome extension designed to simplify nutrition tracking by scanning food webpages, recipes, or grocery items online, instantly providing nutritional data. It then logs the meal, along with its nutritional data, directly into your calendar, allowing you to monitor daily totals such as calories, protein, carbs, and fat. The mission of MacTrac is to empower a healthier lifestyle, incorporating convenience, accuracy, and Artificial Intelligence to make meal tracking simple, efficient, and stress-free.
